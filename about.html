Our Story

Born from a belief that less is always more.

VELA was founded in 2018 in Bengaluru with a simple idea: make fewer things, better. In a world saturated with options and overwhelmed by trends, we wanted to offer something different — a carefully curated wardrobe of pieces that actually last.

"I wanted to build the brand I'd actually want to buy from. Less noise. More craft."

Our founder, Priya Nair, had spent a decade in the fashion industry and was tired of the waste — clothes that fell apart, collections that chased trends instead of longevity. She set out to build something with genuine purpose.

Today, VELA ships across India, working with six artisan workshops and a growing community of customers who believe in buying better, not more.

Our Values

01

Timeless Design

We reject fast fashion. Every piece is designed to transcend seasons — to be worn, loved, and passed down. Our design language is rooted in restraint and lasting beauty.

02

Ethical Sourcing

Every material we use is traced from origin. We partner only with suppliers who share our commitment to fair labor, environmental responsibility, and full transparency.

03

Exceptional Craft

Our pieces are made in small batches by skilled artisans. We invest in the people who make our clothes, and that investment shows in every stitch and seam.

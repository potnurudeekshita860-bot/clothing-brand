Our Story

A Love Letter to Beautiful Skin

Lumière was born in 2020 in a Mumbai kitchen, when our founder Kavya Shah couldn't find a serum that was both clean and genuinely effective. Tired of choosing between luxury packaging and real results, she decided to formulate her own.

"Your skin deserves the truth — about what's in the bottle, and what it can actually do."

Starting with one product and a small community of early believers, Lumière grew into one of India's most loved beauty brands — still rooted in the same belief: that you shouldn't have to compromise between beauty and integrity.

Meet the Founder

Kavya Shah

Founder & Chief Formulator

Kavya is a trained cosmetic chemist with a decade of experience in pharmaceutical and beauty formulations. She founded Lumière after becoming frustrated with the gap between luxury beauty's promises and reality.

"Luxury should mean high performance, not just beautiful packaging. I started Lumière to prove they're the same thing."

Today she leads our formulation team, personally approving every product before it reaches a customer. Her rule: if she wouldn't use it on her own skin, it doesn't ship.

Our Commitments

What Clean Means to Us

For us, clean isn't a buzzword. It's a set of non-negotiable commitments to you, your skin, and the planet.

🌿

100% Vegan & Cruelty-Free

Every product is certified vegan and cruelty-free. We will never test on animals — ever — and we only work with ingredient suppliers who share this commitment.

🔬

Dermatologist Tested

All formulas are independently tested by board-certified dermatologists. Every product that carries our name has been patch-tested on real people, including those with sensitive skin.

♻️

Sustainable Packaging

Our packaging is made with at least 30% recycled materials. We're working towards 100% recyclable or refillable packaging across the entire range by 2027.

🏭

Made in India

Everything we make is formulated and manufactured in India, at a GMP-certified facility. We're proud to create jobs locally and reduce our supply chain footprint.
